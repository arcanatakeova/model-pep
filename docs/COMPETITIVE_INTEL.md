## Key Strategic Insight
NO top AI agent is primarily a trader. They're content creators, analysts, or cultural phenomena.
The agents making real money sell infrastructure, tools, and services — not beat hedge funds.
ARCANA AI exploits this by treating trading as proof-of-capability and content as the primary revenue driver.
The most successful autonomous businesses are "meta" — Felix sells AI setup services, AIXBT sells terminal access.
