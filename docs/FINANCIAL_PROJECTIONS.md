## Month-by-Month Revenue Projections

### Month 1: Foundation ($0-200)
- Revenue: $0-200 (first affiliate clicks, early engagement)
- Costs: ~$300
- Net: -$100 to -$300
- Focus: Build foundation code, start posting, warm up account

### Month 2: Content Traction ($200-800)
- Revenue: X engagement growing, first affiliate conversions, 1-2 consulting inquiries
- Trading: Paper trading (DRY_RUN), learning signal quality
- Costs: ~$350
- Net: -$150 to +$450

### Month 3: First Real Revenue ($500-2,000)
- Revenue: Live trading begins ($100-300 trading P&L), first digital product sale ($29-49 Arcana Playbook), 2-3 affiliate conversions, first UGC client ($50-150)
- First consulting lead qualified and routed to Ian/Tan
- Costs: ~$400
- Net: +$100 to +$1,600

### Month 4-6: Scaling ($2,000-5,000/month)
- Revenue streams active: Trading ($200-500/mo), Digital products ($200-500/mo), Affiliate ($100-300/mo), UGC production ($300-800/mo), First consulting conversion ($2-5K one-time)
- Newsletter at 500+ subscribers
- X followers: 2,000-5,000
- Costs: ~$500
- Net: +$1,500 to +$4,500

### Month 7-9: Compounding ($5,000-15,000/month)
- Trading capital grown to $2,000-3,000 via reinvestment
- Micro-SaaS tools generating $500-2,000 MRR
- Podcast generating ad revenue
- Premium Discord/community ($500-1,500/mo)
- Consulting pipeline: 1-2 deals/month ($3-10K each)
- X followers: 5,000-15,000
- Costs: ~$700
- Net: +$4,300 to +$14,300

### Month 10-12: Autonomous Revenue ($15,000-40,000/month)
- Multiple revenue streams compounding
- Trading: $500-2,000/mo
- Content/products: $2,000-5,000/mo
- Services (UGC, chatbots, SEO): $3,000-10,000/mo
- Consulting: $5,000-15,000/mo
- Programmatic SEO sites: $1,000-5,000/mo
- X followers: 15,000-50,000
- Costs: ~$1,000
- Net: +$14,000 to +$39,000

- Newsletter/content monetization: 10%

### Tier 2: Variable Revenue (20% of income)

### Tier 3: Long-tail/Opportunistic (10% of income)

## Key Assumptions
- Trading wins at 55-60% rate with 2:1 reward:risk
- X account reaches 10K followers in 6 months (aggressive but documented)
- 1-2% of X audience converts to newsletter
- 0.5-1% of newsletter converts to paid product
- Consulting conversion: 1 in 10 qualified leads closes
- UGC clients: 5-10 active clients by month 6
- NO venture funding, NO token launch, pure organic revenue

- Monthly costs: ~$400 average

- 12-month total revenue projection: $80,000-$200,000
