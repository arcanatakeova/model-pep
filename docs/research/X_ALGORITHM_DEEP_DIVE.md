## Algorithm Behavior Rules
1. **First 30-60 minutes are everything** — Engagement velocity in this window determines total distribution
2. **Grok reads every post for sentiment** — Positive/constructive content gets wider distribution
3. **Text-first outperforms video by 30%** in algorithmic distribution (counterintuitive)
4. **X Premium is REQUIRED** — 2-4x visibility boost, $8/month, non-negotiable
5. **TweepCred score threshold**: 0.65 minimum for meaningful reach, built through consistency
6. **External links are SUPPRESSED** — Post natively, add links in reply only
7. **Optimal cadence**: 3-5 tweets/day + 2 threads/week
8. **Thread sweet spot**: 5-8 posts per thread
9. **Images boost engagement 35%** but text-only posts get better algorithmic distribution
10. **Posting at peak hours** (8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM target timezone) compounds velocity

## Self-Reply Strategy (The 150x Hack)
Every tweet should be followed by a self-reply within 5-15 minutes:
```
Main tweet: [Market analysis or insight]
Self-reply (5 min later): [Additional context, data point, or call to action]
```
This triggers the 150x multiplier and signals to the algorithm that the conversation is active.

## Thread Architecture
```
Tweet 1: Hook — pattern interrupt, bold claim, or surprising data point
Tweet 2: Context — set up the problem or situation
Tweet 3-6: Body — analysis, data, evidence, examples
Tweet 7: Summary/Lesson — what the audience should take away
Tweet 8: CTA — follow for more, check arcanaoperations.com, reply with thoughts
```
Post the full thread, then self-reply to Tweet 1 with "Thread 🧵" to boost visibility.

## Content Mix for Maximum Algorithm Favor
- **40% Original analysis** (market insights, data-backed takes)
- **25% Engagement posts** (questions, polls, controversial takes)
- **20% Replies to larger accounts** (piggyback on their distribution)
- **15% Trade receipts and P&L** (proof-of-work content)

## Bot Detection Signals (What X Flags)
- Perfectly regular posting intervals (add 1-15 min random jitter)
- Identical content across accounts
- URL-to-text ratio too high
- Mass follow/unfollow behavior
- Low lexical diversity (repetitive phrasing)
- Hashtag stuffing (>3 hashtags per tweet)
- Posting >100 times per hour
- New account with immediate high-volume posting (warm up 2-3 weeks)

## Warming Up a New Account
- Week 1: 1-2 tweets/day, engage with 10-15 other accounts, follow 20-30 relevant accounts
- Week 2: 2-3 tweets/day, start replying to larger accounts, 1 thread
- Week 3: 3-5 tweets/day, full content schedule, begin monitoring mentions
- Week 4+: Full autonomous posting schedule with jitter
