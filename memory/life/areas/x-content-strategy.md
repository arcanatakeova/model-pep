# X Content Strategy

## The Four Suits
| Suit | Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|------|---------|-----------|
| Wands | Industry Analysis | Build authority | 3-5x daily |
| Cups | Behind-the-Scenes | Show AI capabilities | 2-3x weekly |
| Swords | Case Files | Demonstrate expertise | 2x weekly |
| Pentacles | Product Launches | Drive sales | As needed |

## Algorithm Rules
- Self-reply to own posts: 150x weight (ALWAYS do this)
- No external links in main tweet (add in reply)
- First 30-60 min = make or break for distribution
- X Premium required ($8/mo)
- Random jitter on posting intervals
- Replies are fully autonomous
- 3-5 tweets/day + 2 threads/week

## Content Principles
- Signal, not noise
- No hype language, no rocket emojis
- Pattern-focused, data-driven
- Transparent about failures
- Every post demonstrates consulting capabilities
